Who we are

We're the Indepent Exeter-based practise that's been turning care on it's head.

We believe that hearing plays such a central role in life * , that we want people to have the best hearing they can. Everything we do revolves around this. (Take a look at our Audorama®, and you'll see what we mean.)

What we do

We enable you to MAINTAIN your hearing

Routine hearing checks, expert advice, and honest-to-goodness services like wax-removal and tinnitus taming.

We even offer free half hour consultations ideal for toe-dipping. It gives you the chance to have a chat, find out what we're about, see how we can help.

We enable you to PROTECT your hearing

Custom hearing protection specially designed to keep your hearing safe, we’re like personal bodyguards for musicians, DJs, sound engineers, concert-goers, motorcyclists, swimmers, surfers, and shooters... (Well, for their ears at least.)

We enable you to ENHANCE your hearing

We have some absolutely incredible hearing technology available, some of which you won’t find anywhere else for miles around. More importantly, we know how to get the best out of it.

But that’s not surprising, when you consider how personalised our approach is. Why not take a look at some of our testimonials?

with free client parking, complimentary WIFI, online booking (or by phone), relaxing reception area, beautiful magazines, a warm welcome... and a hot beverage!
